The Process

Three steps. Zero cashiers.

01
Ingredient Detection

As your team builds each order, FastPay's overhead system identifies what goes into the bowl automatically, in real time, with no extra steps from staff.

02
Bill Built in Motion

Each ingredient is added to a running total as the order travels down the line. By the time the guest reaches the end, their bill is ready and waiting.

03
Instant Checkout

Guests tap to pay — card, phone, or cash — at a sleek terminal. No cashier needed. No line. No wait.
