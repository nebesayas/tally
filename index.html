The process

Three steps. Zero cashiers.

01 — DETECT

Read every order

An overhead sensor identifies each ingredient as your team adds it — no scanning, no buttons, no slowdown.

02 — TALLY

Price builds itself

Each item updates a running total down the line. By the time the bowl is closed, the price is already waiting.

03 — PAY

Tap and go

Guests pay by card or phone on your existing POS — no dedicated cashier.* Cash still routes through staff.

Assumptions & methodology
  1. $20/hr cashier cost reflects a New York City wage context and is directional only.
  2. 44,000+ fast casual units excludes QSRs (Technomic Top 500, 2025).
  3. 99% detection accuracy is a target assumption, not an audited multi-location result.

  5. Card and contactless transactions avoid dedicated cashier input; cash may require staff input through your existing POS.
